Warrior:

Offers a balance of strength and dexterity, along with decent starting gear, making it suitable for a versatile physical build.

Knight:
A strong choice for beginners, starting with higher defensive stats and good physical attributes for a melee-focused playstyle.

Swordsman:

Specializes in Dexterity and starts with two weapons, which can be good for players interested in dual-wielding.

Bandit:
Starts with high Strength, making it a good choice for heavy weapons.

Cleric:
Focuses on Faith for miracles and starts with a Mace, offering a different path from pure physical or magical builds.

Sorcerer:
Begins with high Intelligence and Attunement, ideal for spellcasting but with less physical prowess.

Explorer:

A well-rounded class with decent stats, a balanced inventory, and more starting Life Gems, making it a good beginner option.

Deprived:
A challenging class with balanced but low starting stats, no equipment or armor, and a high initial Soul Level.
